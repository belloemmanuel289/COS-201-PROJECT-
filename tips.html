Campus Safety Tips

These simple practices can help you stay safe and reduce risks around campus.

  • Stay alert and pay attention to your surroundings.
  • Avoid isolated routes, especially at night.
  • Move with friends or classmates whenever possible.
  • Do not share personal details with strangers.
  • Keep your phone and valuables secure.
  • Report unusual behavior or suspicious activity immediately.
  • Save emergency numbers on your phone.
  • Use bright and busy routes when moving around campus.
  • Be careful when accepting rides from unfamiliar people.
  • Let someone know your location or travel plan.
