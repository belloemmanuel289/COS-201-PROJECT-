About SIM

Security Incident Mapping System (SIM) is an online platform designed to simplify the reporting of security-related incidents within the university environment. Members of the campus community can report theft, robbery, assault, suspicious activities, emergencies, and other security concerns. Timely reporting helps security personnel respond faster and improve overall campus safety.

Our Mission

To provide a reliable and accessible platform that encourages prompt reporting of security incidents and contributes to creating a safer learning environment.

Our Vision

To become the most trusted campus security reporting platform that promotes peace, safety, and collaboration within the university community.
